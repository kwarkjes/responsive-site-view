About

This Chrome extension helps web designers to take screenshots of their websites at the dimensions they will be seen at on popular mobile devices for demonstration purposes.

Please be aware that this extension does not emulate the devices listed. It simply renders websites via the Chrome browser at the specified dimensions.

The dimensions listed are browser viewport sizes rather than device screen resolutions. These are the dimensions at which the browser would render websites.

You will still need to test your website on lots of real devices. Why not start or contribute to a local device library? See LabUp! for more details..

This extension is by NimbleWorks in association with the Exeter Web device library.
